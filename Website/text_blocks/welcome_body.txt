This is a free, no ads, non-profit, community league and player finding club website, for owners of the realistic table tennis simulation game, Racket Fury VR on the Playstation VR.  
<br><br>
You can find and contact players that are on your local sever, to arrange friendly matches or challenge them in leagues which are specially structured into box divisions to satisfy all playing standards from top players to absolute beginners.  You always have a chance to win in your division as each season renews every three weeks.
<br><br>
By registering, you can add yourself to the player list, take part in leagues, and message other players directly through this website's mediating messaging system (your email address will always remain private) to arrange league or friendly matches.
